# 🚗 Nexusscars

**Nexusscars** is a vehicle export service based in Japan, offering direct access to Japanese car auctions and global shipping. We specialize in exporting high-quality used cars, trucks, vans, campers, and earth-moving equipment worldwide.

🌐 **Live Website**: [nexusscars.com](https://nexusscars.com)

---

## 📦 What We Offer

- ✅ Access to real-time Japan vehicle auctions
- 🚚 Export of used vehicles and machinery from Japan
- 📄 Full support with invoicing, customs, and shipping documents
- 🌍 Worldwide shipping with Bill of Lading preparation
- 💬 Multilingual communication and customer support
